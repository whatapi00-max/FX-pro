Commodity Trading FAQs

Why do traders buy Gold?

Gold is considered a safe-haven asset. Traders buy gold to hedge against inflation, currency devaluation, and economic uncertainty. It tends to rise when stock markets fall.

What drives Gold prices?

Gold prices are influenced by US dollar strength, interest rates, inflation expectations, geopolitical events, and central bank buying and selling activity.
