Swaps & Overnight Fees

Understand how overnight financing charges (swaps) work and view indicative rates for all instruments.

ℹ️ Swaps are applied when a CFD position is held open overnight. Rates are subject to change based on interbank interest rates. Swap-free (Islamic) accounts are available on request.

What Are Swaps?

Everything you need to know about overnight financing charges.

🌙

Overnight Fee

A swap is an interest payment applied when you hold a CFD position open past the daily rollover time (usually 22:00 GMT).

📈

Long vs Short

Swaps can be positive or negative depending on whether you're long or short and the interest rate differential between currencies.

📅

Triple Wednesday

On Wednesdays, swaps are charged at 3x the normal rate to account for the weekend (Saturday and Sunday) when markets are closed.

🕌

Swap-Free Accounts

Islamic accounts are available with no overnight swap charges. Contact our support team to apply for a swap-free account.
