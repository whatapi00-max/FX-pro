Indices Trading FAQs

What is an index CFD?

An index CFD lets you speculate on the price movement of a stock market index like the S&P 500 or FTSE 100 without owning the underlying shares.

What is the difference between Spot and Futures indices?

Spot indices track the real-time index price and carry overnight swap fees. Futures indices have a fixed expiry date and no overnight charges.

Can I trade indices on mobile?

Yes, all index CFDs are available on the fx777 mobile app for iOS and Android with full charting and order management.
