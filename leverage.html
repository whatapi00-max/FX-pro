Leverage & Margin

Understand how leverage works and view the maximum leverage available for each asset class at fx777.

⚠️ Risk Warning: Trading with leverage amplifies both profits and losses. You can lose more than your initial deposit. Please ensure you fully understand the risks before trading with leverage.

What is Leverage?

Leverage allows you to control a large position with a relatively small amount of capital.

🔭

How It Works

With 1:100 leverage, a $1,000 deposit controls a $100,000 position. A 1% move in your favour earns $1,000 (100% return). A 1% move against you loses $1,000 (100% of deposit).

📐

Margin

Margin is the deposit required to open a leveraged position. It is calculated as: Position Size ÷ Leverage Ratio. For a $10,000 trade at 1:100 leverage, margin = $100.

🛡️

Margin Call

If your account equity falls below the margin call level (typically 50%), fx777 will alert you to add funds or close positions to avoid a margin stop-out.

🔴

Stop-Out Level

If your equity falls to the stop-out level (typically 20%), fx777 will automatically close your least profitable open positions to protect the remaining balance.
