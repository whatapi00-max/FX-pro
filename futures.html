Futures Trading FAQs

What are Futures CFDs?

Futures CFDs are contracts that allow you to speculate on the future price of an asset without owning the underlying asset. They mirror real futures contracts with fixed expiry dates.

Do Futures CFDs expire?

Yes, futures CFDs have expiry dates that correspond to the underlying futures contract. fx777 will notify you before expiry and roll open positions to the next contract automatically.

Are there overnight fees on Futures?

No. Unlike spot CFDs, futures CFDs do not carry overnight swap fees. Financing costs are already built into the spread at the time of trading.

What is the difference between Futures and Spot trading?

Spot trading is based on current market prices. Futures trading is based on contracts for a future delivery date. Futures typically have wider spreads but no overnight fees.
