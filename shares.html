Shares CFD FAQs

Do I receive dividends on Share CFDs?

When trading share CFDs, you do not receive physical dividends. However, dividend adjustments are applied to your account on the ex-dividend date.

What are the trading hours for Shares?

Share CFD trading hours follow the underlying exchange. US stocks trade 14:30–21:00 GMT, UK stocks 08:00–16:30 GMT, and EU stocks vary by exchange.

Is there a minimum trade size?

The minimum trade size for share CFDs is 1 unit (1 share) for most instruments, making it accessible for traders of all levels.
