Client Money Protection

fx777 holds all client funds in segregated accounts at top-tier banks, separately from the company's own funds. This ensures your money is protected in the unlikely event of company insolvency. UK clients are also covered by the Financial Services Compensation Scheme (FSCS) up to £85,000.
