What is Forex trading?

Forex (foreign exchange) trading involves buying and selling currency pairs to profit from changes in exchange rates. It is the largest financial market in the world with over $7 trillion traded daily.

Can I trade Forex 24 hours a day?

Yes, the forex market is open 24 hours a day, 5 days a week (Monday to Friday). It follows the sun across the globe through Asian, European and American trading sessions.
